Villages DAO

The Mission

IOT3 represents a visionary convergence of IoT sensors and Helium network hotspots with Solana's blockchain infrastructure. By transforming Helium-connected IoT sensor data into NFTs and integrating Solana wallets within our user console, we empower users and businesses to secure and monetize their data efficiently. Simultaneously, we facilitate decentralized community building by automating reward distribution using Solsplits built into our console back end to hotspot hosts, fostering network growth and engagement. This harmonious fusion promises a landscape where data, connectivity, and blockchain merge to reshape industries and unlock transformative possibilities.

The Solution

Leveraging our IOT3 foundation, we've enhanced the Aerial IoT console to web3, enabling automatic Solana wallet generation for users and facilitating NFT transfers derived from sensor data. With SOLsplits, we ensure fair DePIn device reward distribution. Our Village DAO, anchored on Solana's Realms application, brings transparent governance and treasury management, while our collaboration with the Underdog Protocol empowers us to mint village-representative NFTs, setting the stage for DePIn infrastructure. Prioritizing clean water, our Helium network-based sensors guarantee genuine, blockchain-secured data. This fusion not only connects rural India to web3 economies but also elevates their living standards through innovative sensor applications.
